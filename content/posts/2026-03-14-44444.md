abstract: From May 30 to June 3, we will ride from Paderborn in Germany to Aarhus in Denmark in four days. The route covers 625 kilometers of road cycling with 3,190 meters of elevation gain. The event is organized by the [Rotary Fellowship Cycling to Serve](https://www.cyclingtoserve.org) in collaboration with local cycling clubs and everyone is invited to join! The [Rad-Treff Borchen planned the day-to-day routes](https://rad-treff-borchen.de/projekt-4-444-4/).

This is not only an inspiring cycling event; it is a fantastic way to help! We're aiming to gather donations to buy [444 Buffalo Bikes](https://fund.worldbicyclerelief.org/de-DE/project/rotary-cycling-team?tab=ubersicht&yby6rfrrwwbr7xhah335hpglly=qLswiCWUFUZ1L3g2UXlSmGRfFxunUjgHxUAYBrnGf_VMOr4tOvUqUXbGdTD86ryAv54uklRx) for [World Bicycle Relief](https://worldbicyclerelief.org/). 147 euros will fund a Buffalo Bike for someone in need.

As we arrive in Aarhus, the [4Days of Favrskov](https://4days-favrskov.dk) will begin, and that's another cycling event aiming to contribute to the donation goal. So, the name <span class="inline-block">4 444 4</span> might make more sense now – ride 4 days to help fund 444 bikes, and if you like, take part in the 4Days of Favrskov 😃!

On our ride to Aarhus you’re welcome to join us 🚴 for a day or a part of a day! The route is divided into nine sections with clear meeting spots, so you can easily hop on. Riders with a German Cycling license can even earn official kilometers for their clubs during the event. Though, it's not required to have a license nor to be organized with Rotary. Your passion for road cycling will bring you in.

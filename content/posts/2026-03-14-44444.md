abstract: From May 30th to June 3rd, we will ride within four days from Paderborn in Germany, to Aarhus in Denmark. It´s 618 kilometers road cycling with 2775 meters of elevation. Get the route on [komoot](https://www.komoot.com/de-de/collection/4079156/-444-buffalo-bikes-in-4-tagen?ref=collection).<br>The event is organized by the [Rotary Fellowship Cycling to Serve](https://www.cyclingtoserve.org) and everyone is invited to join!
---

<figure><img src="/img/strael/4days2026.jpg"></figure>

This isn't just an inspiring cycling event; it's a fantastic way to help! We're aiming to gather donations to buy [444 Buffalo Bikes](https://fund.worldbicyclerelief.org/de-DE/project/rotary-cycling-team?tab=ubersicht&yby6rfrrwwbr7xhah335hpglly=qLswiCWUFUZ1L3g2UXlSmGRfFxunUjgHxUAYBrnGf_VMOr4tOvUqUXbGdTD86ryAv54uklRx) for [World Bicycle Relief](https://worldbicyclerelief.org/). Just 147 Euro can help to get one Buffalo Bike. As we arrive in Aarhus, the [4Days of Favrskov](https://4days-favrskov.dk) will begin, and that's another cycling event aiming to contribute to the donation goal. So, the name <span class="inline-block">4 444 4</span> might make more sense now – ride 4 days to help fund 444 bikes and join the 4Days of Favrskov 😃!

You’re welcome to join us 🚴 for just a day or even a part of a day! The route is divided into nine sections with clear meeting spots, so you can easily hop on. Riders with a German Cycling license can even earn official kilometers for their clubs during the event. Though, it´s not required to have a license nor to be organized with Rotary. Your passion for road cycling will bring you in.

[<cite>Modern Font Stacks</cite>](https://modernfontstacks.com) provides beautiful system font stacks that ==don't require downloading of fonts==! The font stacks are organized by typeface classifications, like _System UI, Transitional, Old Style, Humanist, Geometric Humanist, Classical Humanist, Neo-Grotesque, Monospace Slab Serif, Monospace Code, Industrial, Rounded Sans, Slab Serif, Antique, Didone, Handwritten._

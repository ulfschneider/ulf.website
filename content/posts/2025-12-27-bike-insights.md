[<cite>bikeinsights.com</cite>](https://bikeinsights.com) offers a bike geometry database that allows to search and compare bike frames. Their blog article [<cite>How Bikes are Measured</cite>](https://blog.bikeinsights.com/how-bikes-are-measured/) made me aware normalizing the different ways of measuring bikes is absolutely required to come to meaningful comparisons of bike frames.
